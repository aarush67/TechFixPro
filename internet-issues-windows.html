Fix Internet Connectivity Problems on Windows

If you're experiencing issues with your internet connection, try the following steps to resolve the problem.

Back to Blogs

1. Restart Your Router

A simple restart can often fix internet connectivity issues:

Step 1: Unplug your router from the power source.

Step 2: Wait for 30 seconds and plug it back in.

Step 3: Wait for the router to fully reboot, then check the connection again.

2. Run Network Troubleshooter

Windows has a built-in troubleshooter for network issues:

Step 1: Go to Settings and select Update & Security.

Step 2: Click on Troubleshoot and select Internet Connections.

Step 3: Follow the on-screen instructions to resolve the issue.

3. Check Your Network Settings

Ensure your network settings are correctly configured:

Step 1: Open Control Panel and go to Network and Sharing Center.

Step 2: Click on Change adapter settings and check the status of your network connections.

These steps should help resolve internet connectivity problems on Windows.
