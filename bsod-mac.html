Fix Blue Screen of Death (BSOD) on Mac

If your Mac encounters a BSOD, try these steps to fix it.

Back to Blogs

1. Restart Your Mac

Restarting your Mac can resolve most minor issues:

Step 1: Click on the Apple logo in the top left corner and select Restart.

2. Check for Software Updates

Outdated software can sometimes cause BSOD:

Step 1: Go to the Apple menu and select System Preferences.

Step 2: Click Software Update to ensure your Mac is up to date.

3. Reboot into Safe Mode

Booting in Safe Mode can help resolve system issues:

Step 1: Shut down your Mac, then turn it back on and immediately press and hold the Shift key.

Step 2: Release the Shift key once the login window appears and log in to Safe Mode.

Following these steps should help get your Mac back on track without BSOD.
