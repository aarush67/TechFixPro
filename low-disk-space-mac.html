Fix Low Disk Space on Mac

If your Mac is running low on disk space, follow these steps to clean up your system and regain valuable storage space.

Back to Blogs

1. Remove Unused Applications

Uninstalling apps that you no longer use can free up space:

Step 1: Open Finder and go to the Applications folder.

Step 2: Drag unnecessary apps to the Trash.

Step 3: Empty the Trash to permanently remove them from your system.

2. Clear Cache Files

Clearing cache files can help free up storage space:

Step 1: Open Finder and go to Go > Go to Folder.

Step 2: Type ~/Library/Caches and press Enter.

Step 3: Delete all files inside the Caches folder to clear up space.

3. Use Storage Management Tool

macOS has a built-in tool to help you manage your storage:

Step 1: Click the Apple logo and go to About This Mac > Storage.

Step 2: Click on Manage to access the storage management tool.

Step 3: Review large files and move them to an external drive or delete unnecessary files.

Following these steps will help you free up valuable space on your Mac and keep it running smoothly.
