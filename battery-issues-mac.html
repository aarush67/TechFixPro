1. Enable Low Power Mode (macOS 13 and later)

Low Power Mode can extend your battery life:

Step 1: Go to System Settings and select Battery.

Step 2: Turn on Low Power Mode.

2. Check Battery Health

If your battery is degraded, it may be causing rapid drain:

Step 1: Go to System Settings > Battery.

Step 2: Click Battery Health to see the condition of your battery.

3. Manage Energy-Consuming Apps

Some apps can drain your battery quickly. Close unnecessary apps:

Step 1: Open Activity Monitor (found in Applications > Utilities).

Step 2: Identify any high-energy apps and quit them if they're not essential.
