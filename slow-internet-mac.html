Fix Slow Internet Speeds on macOS

If you're experiencing slow internet speeds, try the following steps:

Back to Blogs

1. Restart Your Router

Restarting your router can often resolve connection issues:

Step 1: Unplug your router for 30 seconds.

Step 2: Plug it back in and wait for it to fully reboot.

2. Use Network Diagnostics

macOS has a built-in tool to help diagnose network issues:

Step 1: Go to System Preferences and select Network.

Step 2: Click on Assist me and follow the on-screen instructions.

3. Check for Interference

Interference from other devices can slow down your Wi-Fi:

Step 1: Move your router to a more open space.

Step 2: Avoid placing it near electronics like microwaves or cordless phones.

These steps should help resolve slow internet speeds on macOS.
