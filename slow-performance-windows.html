Fix Slow Performance on Windows

If your Windows PC is running slow, follow these methods to enhance performance.

  • Back to Blogs

1. Disable Unnecessary Startup Programs

Limiting the programs that start when you boot up your system can speed up your PC:

Step 1: Press Ctrl + Shift + Esc to open Task Manager.

Step 2: Click on the Startup tab.

Step 3: Right-click and select Disable on unnecessary programs.

2. Defragment Your Hard Drive

Defragmenting can improve hard drive performance:

Step 1: Open File Explorer and right-click on your C: drive.

Step 2: Select Properties > Tools > Optimize.

Step 3: Click on Optimize to start defragmenting.

3. Check for Malware and Viruses

Run a full system scan to ensure no malicious software is slowing down your system:

Step 1: Open Windows Security from the Start menu.

Step 2: Go to Virus & Threat Protection and select Quick Scan or Full Scan.

Following these steps will help you restore your Windows PC's speed and efficiency.
