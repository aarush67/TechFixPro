Fix Low Disk Space on Windows

If your Windows computer is running out of disk space, follow these methods to free up space and improve your system's performance.

Back to Blogs

1. Uninstall Unnecessary Programs

Removing unnecessary programs can free up significant space:

Step 1: Open the Control Panel.

Step 2: Go to Programs > Programs and Features.

Step 3: Right-click on programs you don't need and select Uninstall.

2. Use Disk Cleanup

Disk Cleanup helps remove temporary files and other data that can free up space:

Step 1: Press Win + R and type cleanmgr.

Step 2: Select your drive and click OK.

Step 3: Check the boxes for temporary files and click OK to clean them up.

3. Move Files to External Storage

If you need more space, consider moving large files like videos to an external hard drive or cloud storage:

Step 1: Connect your external drive or open your cloud storage.

Step 2: Move large files, such as videos and images, to the external drive.

By following these steps, you can free up disk space and improve the performance of your Windows computer.
