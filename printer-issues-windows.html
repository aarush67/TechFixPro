Fix Printer Issues on Windows

If your printer is not working correctly, follow these steps to troubleshoot.

Back to Blogs

1. Restart the Printer and PC

Restarting both your printer and PC can help resolve many printing issues:

Step 1: Turn off your printer and wait for 30 seconds before turning it back on.

Step 2: Restart your computer as well and check if the issue persists.

2. Update Printer Drivers

Outdated printer drivers can cause printing issues:

Step 1: Open Device Manager and find your printer under Printers.

Step 2: Right-click and select Update Driver to make sure itâ€™s up to date.

3. Run the Printer Troubleshooter

Windows has a built-in troubleshooter that can identify and fix printer problems:

Step 1: Go to Settings and select Update & Security.

Step 2: Select Troubleshoot and then Printer to run the troubleshooter.

These steps should help resolve most printer issues on Windows.
