Fix Slow Internet Speeds on Windows

Slow internet speeds can be caused by many factors. Try the following steps to improve your connection speed.

Back to Blogs

1. Restart Your Router

Restarting your router can help improve internet speeds:

Step 1: Unplug your router from the power source for 30 seconds.

Step 2: Plug it back in and wait for it to fully reboot.

2. Run Network Troubleshooter

Windows has a built-in tool for detecting network issues:

Step 1: Go to Settings and click Update & Security.

Step 2: Select Troubleshoot and choose Internet Connections.

3. Check for Interference

Wi-Fi interference from other devices can slow speeds:

Step 1: Move your router to a different location.

Step 2: Avoid placing it near large electronic devices.

These steps should help resolve slow internet speeds on Windows.
