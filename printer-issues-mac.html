Fix Printer Issues on Mac

Follow these steps to resolve printer issues on your Mac.

1. Restart Your Mac and Printer

Restarting both your Mac and printer can solve many problems:

Step 1: Turn off your printer and your Mac, then turn them both back on.

2. Re-add the Printer

Step 1: Go to System Preferences and select Printers & Scanners.

Step 2: Click the + button to add the printer again.

3. Reset Printing System

If all else fails, reset the printing system:

Step 1: Open Printers & Scanners from System Preferences.

Step 2: Right-click in the printer list and select Reset Printing System.

These steps should help resolve most printer issues on Mac.
